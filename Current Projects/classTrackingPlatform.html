About this project -

This platform, built in Streamlit with data storage in AWS S3, is a solution to the challenge of tracking student data and generating progress reports, particularly in educational institutions that intend to personalize instruction as much as possible to individual students. Small schools and tutoring centers benefit especially from this platform.
